My Top 4 Movies of All Time

The Avengers

When Thor evil brother Loki gains access to the unlimited power of the energy cube called the Tesseract, Nick Fury director of SHIELD initiates a superhero recruitment effort to defeat the unprecedented threat to Earth. Joining Fury dream team are Iron Man, Captain America, The Hulk, Thor, The Black Widow and Hawkeye

The Conjuring

In 1970, paranormal investigators and demonologist Lorraine and Ed Warren are summoned to the home of Carolyn and Roger Perron. The Perrons and their five daughters have recently moved into secluded farmhouse, where a supernatural presence has made itself known. Though the manifestations are relatively benign at first, events soon escalate in horrifying fashion, especially after the Warrens discover the house's macabre history

The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe

While playing, Lucy and her siblings find a wardrobe that lands them in mystical place called Narnia. Here they realized that it was fated and they must now unite Aslan to defeat an evil queen

Harry Potter

An orphaned boy enrolls in a school of wizardry, where he learns the truth about himself, his family and the terrible evil that haunts the magical world
